American

This is a dish of charred sausage. It's a very simple dish, yet packed with flavour and texture. It's a dish that originated from texas (who are known for their BBQ). This is how you can make the dish: (5 sausages beef/chicken)

Ingridients
1. Salt (1 tsp)
2. Pepper (1 tsp)
3. Red Chilli Powder (1 tsp)
5. Honey (1 tsp)
6. Coconut oil (1 tsp)

Directions
1. Pre-heat the oven to 220 degrees (celcius)
2. Marinate the 5 sausages with all the ingridients
3. Immediatley put the sausages in the oven for 20 minutes
4. Remove the sausages from the oven and add a glaze of butter over each sausage


African

This dish is a traditional dish in Tanzania, it's called Jerk Chicken. It's a extra grilled skin chicken with a marination that uses salt, pepper and vegetable oil (glaze). This is the recipe for the dish: (1 whole chicken w/ skin)

Ingridients
1. Salt (1 tsp)
2. Pepper (1 tsp)
3. Vegetable oil (1 tsp)

Directions
1. Start off by washing the chicken
2. Rub the salt and pepper aross the chicken's skin
3. Grab a kitchen brush and apply the oil on the chicken
4. Pre-heat the oven to 220 degrees (celcius)
5. Put the chicken in the oven for 30 mins
6. Remove the chicken and let it cool down for 5 mins
7. Add the chicken back for 20 mins and 180 degrees (celcius)


Indian

The following dish is a corn flour and yoghurt edible that is a soft, flavour packed sponge cake that is a bit spicy to the taste. It's called "Dhokdha". This is how the dish is made: (10 cubes)

Ingridients
1. Salt (2 tps)
2. Pepper (tps)
3. 2 green chillis
4. 1 cup yoghurt
5. 1 1/2 cup corn flour
6. Corriander (handfull)

Directions
1. Mix all the ingridients together to form a paste
2. Grab a greesed pan and pour the paste in the pan
3. Add the pan in a pot of boiling water in order to steam the edible. (Add 1 1/2 cup of water)
4. Steam the edible to 1 to 1 1/2 hours
5. Remove the pan and let it cool for 20 mins before serving
